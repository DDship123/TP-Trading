Biography

Truong Pham, widely known in the community as “TP Trading”, is a living testament to perseverance and resilience in the tough world of finance.

In 2020, Truong entered the trading market for the first time with a small amount of borrowed capital. However, due to a lack of experience, combined with greed and unrealistic expectations, he quickly faced bankruptcy, losing everything after just a few months of failed attempts in the Forex market.

But that failure became a powerful turning point. Truong refused to give up. Over the next two years, he committed himself to serious learning — reading hundreds of books, practicing on demo accounts, and sharpening his risk management skills with strict discipline.

By 2022, Truong returned to the market with a clear strategy, solid discipline, and strong trading psychology. Within just two years, he turned a $10,000 account into over $150,000 in profit, thanks to precise trades in currency pairs and commodity indices.
